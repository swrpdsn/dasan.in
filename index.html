About Adv. Dasan

Adv. Khandappa Dasan is a seasoned public servant, former State Executive Member (BJP SC Morcha, Karnataka), Ex‑Zilla Panchayat Member, and the Founder‑Chairman of Nivedita Educational Trust. His life’s work spans grassroots governance, social reform, and institution-building across Yadgir and beyond.

Guided by a simple creed — dignity, opportunity, and education for all — he has led initiatives that opened doors for the marginalized, especially SC/ST communities.

  • Founder & Chairman — Nivedita Educational Trust (6 institutions)
  • Ex‑State Executive Member — BJP SC Morcha, Karnataka
  • Ex‑Member — Zilla Panchayat, Gulbarga; Ex‑Municipal Councillor, Yadgir
  • Awards — Karnataka Ratna Shree; Best Politician Award; Senior Citizen Award
